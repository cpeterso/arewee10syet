Are your add-ons e10s compatible?

To test an add-on:

  1. Install Firefox Nightly.
  2. Confirm that the add-on works in Firefox Nightly as expected.
  3. Use the File menu or "hamburger" toolbar button to open a new e10s window.
  4. Confirm that the add-on still works in the e10s window as expected.

If the add-on works as expected, then click the add-on's "it works" link below to tell Mozilla!

If the add-on behaves differently in the e10s window, then click the add-on's "Report bug" link below. Please include a description of what you expected to happen, what actually happened, and steps to reproduce the problem.
